Mission 

The mission of the ADP Social Work Caucus is to empower the social work community to advocate for all vulnerable populations and promote social justice; to encourage social workers to become civically engaged and seek public office; to work in support of social work values and the Democratic Party candidates who champion those values; to elect Democrats; and, to support and strengthen the Arizona Democratic Party.
