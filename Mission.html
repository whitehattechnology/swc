Mission

The Arizona Democratic Party LGBT Caucus supports policy and legislation that is provides protection and draws inclusion of the LGBTQ+ Community both locally, statewide, and Nationally. We support LGBTQ+ and/or strong Ally candidates. Your LGBT Caucus is working to increase visibility, increase membership, and have a long-lasting impact throughout our beautiful state of Arizona. We look forward to working with you!
